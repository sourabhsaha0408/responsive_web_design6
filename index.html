FEATURED DESTINATIONS
LONDON
London, the capital of England, is a city rich in history, culture, and landmarks. Known for its iconic landmarks such as Big Ben, Buckingham Palace, and the London Eye, it's no surprise that London is one of the most visited cities in the world.
NEW YORK
You have probably heard this familiar for this term for New York. Originally, this name was first used in a horse racing column in a 1920s newspaper. Significantly, the term “Big Apple” was used to describe a big money prize at important horse races held around the city.
KOLKATA
The Port of Kolkata is India's oldest operating port. Four Nobel laureates and two Nobel Memorial Prize winners are associated with the city. Though home to major cricketing venues and franchises, Kolkata stands out in India for being the country's centre of association football.
IMPORTANT TRAVEL TIPS
Learn about the local culture, customs, safety, and attractions. You can find information from travel blogs, local newspapers, and travel websites
Booking in advance can help you avoid last-minute price increases.
Packing light can help you move around more easily and get through baggage claim faster.
Make copies of your passport, travel insurance certificate, and medical prescriptions. This will make it easier to replace them if they are lost
